The New Dawn Story

New Dawn Doula Services is a Calgary-based business providing birth planning and support for women and partners in the greater Calgary area since 2014. We believe that every mother should have the benefit of a doula's support whether they are expecting their first child or fifth.

Hiring a doula to support you during child birth ensures you will have a professional on-hand who is dedicated to you and advocating your wishes.

Studies show that continuous one-to-one support results in fewer caesarean births and more satisfied mothers.
